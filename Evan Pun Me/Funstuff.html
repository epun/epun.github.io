Work + Fun

Dungeons and Diabetes    A hackathon project for the 2014 Health 2.0 Codeathon. We want to create a tool for people to help manage the comorbidity of diabetes and depression. This will be a simple patient care managment platform where users can keep track of their medical infomration and perscriptions. The platform gamifies the experience for the user by earning points for adhearence that they can use to upgrade their avatar or trade in for real world health and fitness items.
