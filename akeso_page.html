Akeso

Akeso is a platform to help families prevent and manage type 2 diabetes together. The project was a finalist the Berkeley-Haas Healthcare Conference design challenge sponsored by UCSF and IDEO.

Problem

Type 2 Diabetes is a lifestyle disease that is difficult to prevent and manage without having a supportive system in place. Current solutions are lacking in providing a comprehensive platform for both prevention and management of the disease. Most importantly, these solutions are not empowering the family unit as a whole: Diabetes is not an individual, isolated disease. Additionally, the national cost for diabetes management is astronomical.

Solution

We created a two-part solution, a companion app and a subscription box, to deliver the following: Family participation, Professional support, Tailored information, Convenient management tools

User Research

After interviewing patients with diabetes and healthcare professionals with expertise in treating this chronic disease, we found a common theme of "family" present in all treatment and management strategies. This finding is reflected throughout our product.

Visual Branding

In creating a family-oriented digital health solution, we wanted branch out from the traditional "medical blue", which conveys professionalism and confidence, to include a warmer color for family-friendliness. The selected typography is also less professional and more welcoming to reinforce the idea of family-friendliness.
