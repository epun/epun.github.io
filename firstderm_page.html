First Derm

First Derm is a teledermatology company I work with. The feature product that we developed was mobile and web app where useres could anonymously ask a dermatologist about any skin condition. The user would take two photos, type a brief description of their condition and meidcal history, and have an answer within hours. The app had over 100K downloads, was available in 6 languages, and had over 25K a month in revenues. For this project I led product design for both ios and android apps, as well as all the logos and branding.
